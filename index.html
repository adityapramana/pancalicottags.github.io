About Pancali Cottage

Situated in Nusa Lembongan, a few steps from Dream Beach, Pancali Cottages features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a garden. Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The air-conditioned rooms provide pool view and come with a desk and free WiFi. At the guest house, rooms include a balcony. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Pancali Cottages also boast a garden view. The rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Popular points of interest near Pancali Cottages include Sandy Bay Beach, Mushroom Bay and Devil's Tear. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International, 88 km from the guest house, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
